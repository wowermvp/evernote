名词（n.），冠词
名词的功用：当主词、补语、受语
1、名词的种类
普通名词=>book,pencil,dog,spaceship...
集合名词=>class,family,audience...
例：
  1. My family is large.
  2. My family are all early risers.
专有名词=>Bob,Smith,April,London...
*下列专有名词需要加上定冠词the
例：the United States. the United Nations
物质名词=>glass,wood,paper,butter,fruit...
*数字+容器（度量衡）+物质名词
例：
  1. a loaf of bread
  2. a cup of coffee
  3. a sheet of paper
  4. a spoonful of sugar
抽象名词=>beauty,honesty,love,patience,happiness,music...等。（不可数名词）
2、名词的数=>个数1：单数/个数2以上=>复数
规则变化的复数名词
名词字尾s=>大部分名词
例：dog-dogs/book-books/gril-grils
名词字尾为s,sh,ch,x,o=>加es
例：class-classes/bus-buses/dish-dishes/bench-benched/box-boxes
（注意）名词字尾为子音+o，复数名词加es，如tomatoes，但有例外：photos,pianos等。
名词字尾为子音+y=>去y+ies
例：baby-babies/story-stories/city-cities/lady-ladies
名词字尾为f或fe=>去f或fe+ves
例：leaf-leaves/wife-wives/knife-knives
*例外：handkerchiefs,chiefs,roofs
不规则变化的复数名词
名词字尾为en,ren
例：ox-oxen/child-children
改变母音
例：man-men/woman-women/goose-geese/tooth-teeth/mouse-mice
单复数同形
例：fish,deer,sheep,Chinese,Japanese
*one fish/two fish 一条鱼/两条鱼
*a kind of fish 一种鱼
*two kinds of fishes 两种鱼
3、名词的所有格
单数名词=>名词's
例：The boy's schoolbag/Joan's dress
复数名词=>名词'(直接加一撇)
例：a girls' school/these students' teacher
字尾非s的复数名词=>名词's
例：children's playground
特别注意的所有格用法
共同所有格及个别所有格
*共同所有格=>名词+名词+......+名词's
 个别专有格=>名词's+名词's+.....+名词's
例：
  1. Harry and Bill's father is a scientist.
  2. Harry's and Bill's fathers are scientists.
（无）生物所有格A的B=>B of A
例：
  1. 桌子的脚    the legs of the table
  2. 车门    the door of the car
  3. 女孩的名字    the girl's name/the name of girl

所有格之后的名词，如在句中非常容易理解时，可省略。
例：
  1. she's going to the dentist's.
  2. I met him at the barber's.
  3. We like to eat lunch at McDonald's.
4、冠词
冠词可分为不定冠词a/an及定冠词the，它通常放名词前，用来修饰名词
a/an的用法=>a + 子音开头的单数名词
            an + 母音开头的单数名词
例：a book,a girl,a young man
    an apple,an umbrella,an old woman
a/an的发音=>a[ə],an[ən];强调时，a[e],an[æ]
the的用法=>母音前，读[ðI];子音前，读[ðə]。
例：
  1. Please shut the door.
  2. The rich aren't always happy.
*the + 形容词泛指“...的人”，代表复数。

be动词，一般动词的现在式
be动词表示状态和存在
1、be动词的现在式---am,are,is
be动词的含义
    是---> 例：We are happy.
    在---> 例：She is in America.
主词+be动词
    第一人称(I) + am => I am a boy.
    第二人称(you)/复数名词 + are => You are my sons.
    第三人称单数/单数名词 + is => He is my student.
be动词的否定=> am/are/is + not
    肯定句：主词 + am/are/is...... => He is a good baseball player.
    否定句：主词 + am/are/is + not...... => He is not a good baseball player.

be动词的疑问句=> 将be动词放在主词前，句尾加问号。
    肯定句：主词 + am/are/is ...
    否定句：Am/Are/Is + 主词 ...? （....吗？）
    例：That is his camera. => Is that his camera?
        The girl is a junior high school student. => Is the girl a junior high school student?
*be动词开头的疑问句，可用Yes或No回答。
    问句：Am/Are/Is + 主词 ...?
    答句：Yes,主词 + am/are/is. No,主词 + am/are/is + not.
    例：Is that man your math teacher?
        Yes,he is./No,he is not.

2、一般动词的现在式
主词 + 一般动词=>现在式中，主词为第三人称单数，一般动词加s或es。
第一人称：I like dogs./We like dogs.
第二人称：You like dogs./You like dogs.
第三人称：He likes dogs./They like dogs.
*一般动词加s或es的方法
    a.大部份动词加s，其发音为[S]或[Z]。
    例：works[S] plays[Z]
    b.一般动词字尾为o,s,sh,ch,时，加es。
    例：goes[Z] washes[IZ]
    c.一般动词字尾为子音+y时，须去y加ies。
    例：cry->cries study->studies
*have和has => have（有；吃）的单数动词为has。
例：They have a lot of money.
    He has a lot of money.
一般动词的否定句 => 不可在一般动词的后面加not，须用助动词do/does。
*do => 用于主词为I,you,复数/does => 用于主词第三人称单数
    肯定句：主词 + 一般动词 ...
    否定句：主词 + do/does + not + 原形动词 ...
    例：1、The twin brothers go to school by bus.
        否定句：The twin brothers do not go to school by bus.
        2、Sam has dinner at the restaurant.
        否定句：Sam does not have dinner at the restaurant.
*比较：否定句
    be动词 => He is my boyfriend.
              He isn't my boyfriend.
    一般动词 => He likes dogs.
                He doesn't like dogs.
一般动词的疑问句 => 不可将一般动词放主词前，须用助动词do/does。
疑问句：Do/Does + 主词 + 原形动词 ...?
例：1、You visit your grandmother on Sundays.
    问句：Do you visit your grandmonther on Sundays?
    2、He comes from England.
    问句：Does he come from England?
*助动词do或does开头的问句，Yes/No之后，用do/does回答。
例：Does the little boy go to school?
    答句：Yes,he does./No,he doesn't.
*比较：疑问句
be动词 => she is beautiful.
          Is she beautiful?    Yes,she is./No,she isn't.
一般动词 => she loves tennis.
            Does she love tennis?  Yes,she does./No,she doesn't.
